# Cin7 Core – Smart Inventory Management & Multi‑Channel Fulfillment Tool

**Cin7 Core** is a powerful, flexible inventory management solution designed to help product‑based businesses organize stock, track orders, and achieve operational efficiency.  
With **Cin7 Core**, you can transform chaotic inventory spreadsheets into structured workflows.  
**Cin7 Core** supports demand forecasting, automated reorder points, and cross‑channel synchronization, making it an essential operations companion for retailers, wholesalers, and manufacturers.

## About Cin7 Core

**Cin7 Core** combines the simplicity of a product catalog with the power of an order management system. Unlike basic inventory tools, **Cin7 Core** offers deep customization: custom product attributes, bin locations, lot tracking, and serial numbers.  
**Cin7 Core** works seamlessly with your existing sales channels (Shopify, Amazon, eBay, Etsy) and provides real‑time updates across warehouses.  
Whether you manage a single store or a global supply chain, **Cin7 Core** adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.  
**Cin7 Core** respects your privacy – all data stays under your control with optional role‑based access.  
With **Cin7 Core**, you gain clarity, reduce stockouts, and fulfill more orders every day.

## Key Features of Cin7 Core

- **Inventory Optimization** – **Cin7 Core** automatically calculates reorder points and economic order quantities.  
- **Multi‑Channel Sync** – **Cin7 Core** reads and writes orders from Shopify, Amazon, eBay, and Etsy.  
- **Custom Product Attributes** – Create saved views inside **Cin7 Core** for variants, bundles, and kits.  
- **Batch Updating** – Update multiple SKUs at once using **Cin7 Core** batch mode.  
- **Recurring Purchase Orders** – Set weekly or monthly auto‑POs with **Cin7 Core**.  
- **Attachments & Files** – Add product images, safety data sheets, and certificates in **Cin7 Core**.  
- **Team Collaboration** – Assign pick lists and packing tasks via **Cin7 Core**.  
- **Time Tracking** – Log warehouse labor hours directly inside **Cin7 Core** for cost analysis.  
- **Dark Mode & Dashboards** – Use **Cin7 Core** with system‑wide dark theme and customizable widgets.  
- **Export & Backup** – Export your **Cin7 Core** data to CSV, XML, or PDF.  
- **Cross‑Platform** – Use **Cin7 Core** on web, iOS, and Android.  
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** – Navigate **Cin7 Core** faster with hotkeys.  
- **Push Notifications** – Get alerts from **Cin7 Core** when stock falls below threshold.  
- **Templates** – Save bill of materials (BOM) structures inside **Cin7 Core** for reuse.  
- **Search & Replace** – Find any SKU instantly using **Cin7 Core** advanced search.
